Nothing Phone 3a Pro Review: Unique Design Meets Performance

ProZiphy April 11, 2025 • 5 min read

The Nothing Phone 3a Pro is the latest addition to the mid-range smartphone market, bringing a unique design and solid performance at an affordable price point. Known for its transparent aesthetics and innovative features, Nothing Technology Limited has once again delivered a device that stands out in a crowded field. This review dives deep into the Nothing Phone 3a Pro, exploring its design, display, performance, cameras, battery life, and overall value.

A Transparent Design That Stands Out

One of the defining features of the Nothing Phone series is its transparent design, and the 3a Pro continues this tradition with subtle refinements. The phone's back panel showcases intricate components illuminated by customizable Glyph lighting. These lights can be programmed to convey notifications or charging status, adding a futuristic touch to the device.

Measuring 163.5 mm in height and weighing 201 grams, the Nothing Phone 3a Pro feels solid yet comfortable to hold. Available in black, white, and blue color options, it appeals to users who value both style and durability.

Display: Bright and Responsive

The Nothing Phone 3a Pro features a stunning 6.77-inch OLED display with a resolution of 2392 x 1080 pixels and a peak brightness of 3,000 nits. The adaptive refresh rate of up to 120Hz ensures smooth animations and fluid scrolling, making it ideal for gaming and multimedia consumption.

Whether indoors or outdoors, the display remains vibrant and easy to view under various lighting conditions. Scratch-resistant Panda Glass adds durability to the screen, ensuring protection against everyday wear and tear.

Performance: Powered by Snapdragon

Under the hood, the Nothing Phone 3a Pro is powered by Qualcomm's Snapdragon 7s Gen 3 chipset paired with an Adreno 710 GPU. This combination delivers reliable performance for multitasking, gaming, and AI-driven tasks. With options for 8GB or 12GB of RAM and storage configurations of up to 256GB, users can choose a model that suits their needs.

Running on Android 15 with NothingOS 3.1, the phone promises three years of Android upgrades and six years of security patches. This longevity ensures that users can enjoy a secure and up-to-date experience for years to come.

Cameras: A Photographer’s Delight

The Nothing Phone 3a Pro boasts an impressive triple-camera setup on the rear:

  • Main Camera: A 50MP sensor with a Type 1/1.56 sensor paired with an f/1.9 aperture delivers sharp images with excellent detail.
  • Telephoto Camera: A Sony Lytia LYT-600 sensor offers a 50MP resolution with a 3x optical zoom lens capable of up to 60x ultra zoom.
  • Ultra-Wide Camera: An 8MP sensor provides a wide field of view at 120 degrees.

The front-facing camera is equally impressive with a high-resolution 50MP sensor for selfies and video calls.

The phone’s AI-powered photography features enhance image quality by optimizing HDR settings, skin tones, night shots, and saturation levels.

Battery Life: Built for Longevity

The Nothing Phone 3a Pro is equipped with a robust 5,000mAh battery that supports fast wired charging at up to 50W. Users can achieve a full charge in just under an hour (56 minutes). Wireless charging is also supported at speeds up to 15W.

In real-world usage scenarios involving gaming, streaming, and multitasking, the battery easily lasts through an entire day without requiring frequent recharges.

Software Experience: Clean and Intuitive

The phone runs on NothingOS 3.1 atop Android 15. The interface is clean and intuitive while incorporating AI-driven features like Essential Space—a tool for organizing photos, notes, documents, and other files efficiently.

With regular updates promised for three years alongside extended security patches for six years, users can trust that their device will remain secure and functional over time.

Price and Availability

The Nothing Phone (3a) Pro is priced at $459 globally (approximately INR ₹30,000), making it an affordable option compared to competitors like the Google Pixel series or OnePlus mid-range devices. Its availability may be limited in certain regions due to enrollment requirements in beta programs or supply constraints.

The Verdict: Is It Worth Buying?

If you’re looking for a mid-range smartphone that combines unique aesthetics with solid performance, the Nothing Phone (3a) Pro is an excellent choice. Its transparent design sets it apart from competitors while delivering reliable hardware specifications at an affordable price point.

The phone excels in areas like camera quality, display brightness, battery life, and software updates—making it suitable for both casual users and tech enthusiasts alike.
