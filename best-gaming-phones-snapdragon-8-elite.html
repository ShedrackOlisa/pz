Best Gaming Phones with Snapdragon 8 Elite Chipsets

ProZiphy April 11, 2025 • 5 min read

Mobile gaming has reached new heights in 2025, and Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset is at the forefront of this revolution. Offering unparalleled performance, advanced graphics capabilities, and efficient power consumption, the Snapdragon 8 Elite has become the go-to choice for gamers looking for the best experience on handheld devices. In this article, we’ll explore the top gaming phones powered by this cutting-edge chipset, highlighting their features, benchmarks, and real-world performance.

What Makes the Snapdragon 8 Elite a Game-Changer?

The Snapdragon 8 Elite is built on TSMC’s advanced 3nm process node and features Qualcomm’s Oryon CPU architecture. This chipset delivers significant improvements over its predecessors:

  • CPU Performance: The Oryon CPU boasts two Prime cores clocked at up to 4.32 GHz, offering a 45% boost in single-core and multi-core performance compared to the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3.
  • GPU Capabilities: The Adreno 830 GPU provides up to a 40% improvement in graphics processing and a 35% enhancement in ray tracing capabilities.
  • AI Integration: Equipped with Qualcomm’s Hexagon AI Engine, the chipset supports on-device multimodal AI for smarter gaming experiences.
  • Thermal Efficiency: Optimized power consumption ensures longer gaming sessions without overheating.

These advancements make the Snapdragon 8 Elite ideal for running high-end games, emulating classic titles, and delivering smooth performance across demanding applications.

Top Gaming Phones with Snapdragon 8 Elite Chipsets

Here are some of the best smartphones powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset in 2025:

  • Realme GT7 Pro: A budget-friendly option that delivers flagship-level gaming performance.
  • Xiaomi Black Shark 6 Pro: Designed specifically for gamers with advanced cooling systems and customizable controls.
  • Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate: A powerhouse with dedicated gaming features like AirTriggers and enhanced audio systems.
  • Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra: Combines premium design with exceptional gaming capabilities.
  • Nubia RedMagic 9 Pro: Offers cutting-edge features like a built-in cooling fan and high refresh rate displays.

Realme GT7 Pro

The Realme GT7 Pro is one of the most affordable smartphones equipped with the Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset. Priced at under $600, it offers impressive gaming performance without compromising on quality. The phone features a vibrant AMOLED display with a refresh rate of up to 120Hz, ensuring smooth visuals during gameplay.

The GT7 Pro excels in benchmarks such as Geekbench (3,033 single-core, 9,271 multi-core) and AnTuTu (2.75 million points). Its efficient thermal management allows extended gaming sessions without overheating.

Xiaomi Black Shark 6 Pro

The Xiaomi Black Shark series has long been a favorite among mobile gamers, and the Black Shark 6 Pro continues this legacy. Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset, it features advanced cooling systems that prevent thermal throttling during intense gameplay.

The phone includes a customizable physical trigger system for precise control in FPS games like Call of Duty Mobile and PUBG Mobile. Its high-refresh-rate AMOLED display ensures minimal input lag and vibrant visuals.

Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate

The Asus ROG Phone series is synonymous with mobile gaming excellence, and the ROG Phone 7 Ultimate takes it to another level. With its Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, this device delivers unmatched performance across demanding games and emulation tasks.

The phone includes unique features such as AirTriggers (touch-sensitive shoulder buttons), an advanced cooling system (AeroActive Cooler), and superior audio quality for immersive gameplay. Its benchmarks consistently rank among the highest in its class.

Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultrae

The Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra combines premium design with exceptional gaming capabilities. While primarily marketed as a flagship smartphone for general use, its Snapdragon 8 Elite processor makes it a formidable choice for gamers seeking smooth performance across all applications.

The phone’s large AMOLED display supports HDR10+ for stunning visuals during gameplay. Its battery life is optimized for extended sessions thanks to efficient power management by Qualcomm’s chipset.

Nubia RedMagic 9 Pro

The Nubia RedMagic series is known for pushing boundaries in mobile gaming technology. The RedMagic 9 Pro features a built-in cooling fan that keeps temperatures low even during marathon gaming sessions.

The phone’s display supports refresh rates of up to 165Hz—perfect for fast-paced games requiring quick reactions. With its Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, it handles demanding titles effortlessly while maintaining stable frame rates.

The Verdict: Which Gaming Phone Should You Choose?

Your choice depends on your priorities:

  • If affordability matters most: Choose the Realme GT7 Pro for excellent value under $600.
  • If you want dedicated gaming features: Opt for Xiaomi Black Shark or Nubia RedMagic models.
  • If you prefer premium design: Go for Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra or Asus ROG Phone Ultimate.

No matter which device you select from this lineup of Snapdragon 8 Elite-powered phones in 2025, each offers unique advantages tailored to different user needs.
