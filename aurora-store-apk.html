Open Source Vibes Only

This alone is a game-changer. The Play Store may not be the worst with ads, but Aurora doesn’t play that game at all. No ads. No weird sponsored stuff. Just apps. It’s refreshing. Like opening a fridge and finding it stocked with only your favorite snacks.
